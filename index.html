FAQ

How much does a day pass cost?

Just need a place to work out for the day? We have a $20 day pass you can purchase at any club during staffed hours.

What If I don’t know what to do at the gym?

Not sure what to do when you are at the gym? We can help with that! Every Club Fitness member gets a FREE session with a personal trainer which we call our “Fit Plan”. This plan is a road map designed for YOU to reach your goals and would be a great starting point for your fitness journey. If you ever have any questions, we have the largest team of certified personal trainers in the St. Louis area ready to help you.

How do I change my membership information?

You may update memberships in person at any location or by logging into your account online. You must register your account first to access your account. If you want to manage multiple memberships, you will have to do the same for each membership.

How do I get a new FIitZone access card?

New cards can be purchased at the front desk of any location during staffed hours. If you still have your card but need a new one due to wear and tear, there is no charge when turning in your old card.
